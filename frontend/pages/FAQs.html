    Orders & Payments

    How do I place an order and what payment methods are accepted?

    One of many ways of ordering is by going to the Gallery page and looking for the item you like and then clicking for the order button, and as of now the only payment method available is COD.

    Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

    Yes, you can cancel your order as long as its not yet on "ready to be delivered" status. for modification on the other hand, you need to cancel it first and order again.
